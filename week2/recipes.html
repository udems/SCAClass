Chocolate Bread

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti - 200g
  • Olive Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Cherry Tomatoes - 1 cup, halved
  • Fresh Basil - 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black Pepper - to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add spaghetti and cook according to package instructions, until al dente.
  3. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  5. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the skillet.
  8. Toss to combine with the tomato mixture.
  9. Remove from heat and stir in chopped fresh basil.
  10. Serve hot, optionally garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.
